about

I’m a designer and technologist based in Naarm / Melbourne, b. 1999 in Aotearoa New Zealand. My practice works across audio-reactive visuals, interactive installation, and motion, building real-time systems for touring artists, brands, and cultural institutions.

I hold a Bachelor of Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Auckland, and worked as a software developer before moving into visual design — an engineering background that shapes how I build shows: versioned, tested, and performed live. I work primarily in TouchDesigner, GLSL, Python, and depth-sensing hardware.

Recent work includes visual design for The xx, Nine Inch Nails, Nike, Odetari, Ravyn Lenae, Its Murph, and smokedope2016, in collaboration with studios including MTLA Studio, 1080p Studios, Phase 3 Concepts, and Lyrical Lemonade. I’m a co-founder of Touch Collective, a creative-technology studio and workshop series, and I teach TouchDesigner through workshops, talks, and published tutorials.

I’m available for commissions, art direction, teaching, and speaking. A full record of projects, performances, awards, and press is on the CV.
