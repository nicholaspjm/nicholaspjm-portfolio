about

Nicholas Marriott is a creative technologist based in Naarm / Melbourne, b. 1999 in Aotearoa New Zealand. He works in real-time systems as a visual designer, performer, and exhibiting artist, across of variety of mediums.

His visuals have featured on major stages and screens, including The xx's 2026 festival tour and a Grammy winning music video for Nine Inch Nails, alongside commercial work for Nike and live performances at festivals including Pitch Music & Arts. His installation work extends these systems into physical space, most recently a large scale reactive light environment for Chadstone. His practice has been profiled in It's Nice That and Rolling Stone.

I’m available for commissions, art direction, teaching, and speaking.

I’m a creative technologist working across audio-reactive visuals, interactive installation, and real-time systems, based in Naarm / Melbourne.

My practice sits between software engineering and live performance, building responsive systems for touring artists, brands, and cultural institutions. Recent work spans stage and festival visual design, music-video VFX, and interactive installation.

tools ~/practice/released

  • 01/09blob tracker. TouchDesigner-native blob tracking component for interactive floors and walls. (TouchDesigner · .tox) ↗
  • 02/09lumen. Live-coding environment for DMX lighting rigs. Write patterns live and the lights follow on the next beat. (TypeScript · WebSocket · DMX) ↗
  • 03/09live coding. Live-coding environment for real-time visuals. Write, hear, and see the patch change while it runs. (TouchDesigner · Python) ↗
  • 04/09wiki visualiser. Turns Wikipedia into real-time visual maps from articles, link graphs, and live edits. (TypeScript · WebGL) ↗
  • 05/09datamosh. Datamoshing as an instrument. Compression-artifact glitching made controllable and performable. (TouchDesigner · GLSL) ↗
  • 06/09palette chooser. Real-time palette extraction using k-means colour analysis, turning any image or live feed into usable swatch palettes. (TouchDesigner · Python) ↗
  • 07/09TD-Notes-Tools. Two external .tox note panels for TouchDesigner: a notebook and a sticky canvas that link to and jump to nodes. (TouchDesigner · Python) ↗
  • 08/09td-ascii-patterns. ASCII pattern generators for TouchDesigner compositions. (TouchDesigner · GLSL) ↗
  • 09/09claude-ableton-visualizer. Ableton Live session data piped into AI-assisted real-time visuals. (Python · Max for Live) ↗
