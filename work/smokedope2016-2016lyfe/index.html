2025 · commissioned · music video / vfx

2016LYFE, smokedope2016

Official music video visual design for smokedope2016 via Lyrical Lemonade. Gaussian splat environments built from 3D scans. Visual design.

For Lyrical Lemonade's premiere of smokedope2016's “2016LYFE”, I created a series of Gaussian splat visuals built from 3D scans in TouchDesigner. Working with 3D artists, we turned entire scenes into splats that grow, dissolve, and morph between moments.

The result is a visual language that feels alive. Scenes reconstructed from real space, then made to breathe and come apart in ways only real-time tooling makes possible.
